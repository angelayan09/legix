Welcome to Legix

Turning legal texts into easy-to-read explanations

About:

Our mission at Legix, is to make government documents accessible and understandable for everyone. By turning dense bills, contracts, and legal texts into clear summaries. We remove the barriers of legal jargon that often leave people confused or excluded. We believe simplifying legal documents is essential for transparency, civic engagement, and empowering individuals to make informed decisions that impact their lives and communities.
